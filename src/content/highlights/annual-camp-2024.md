Our annual camp brought together cadets from across the company for three days of intensive training, character building, and fellowship. The camp featured outdoor activities, leadership workshops, and spiritual sessions that challenged our young men to grow in faith, discipline, and service.

## Highlights

- Rock climbing and team building activities
- Evening devotions and fellowship
- Leadership training sessions
- Survival skills workshops
- Award ceremonies recognizing outstanding cadets

The camp was a tremendous success, with cadets leaving inspired and equipped to serve their communities better.
