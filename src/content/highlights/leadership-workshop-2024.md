Our senior cadets attended a specialized leadership workshop designed to develop their mentorship and management skills. The workshop covered communication, conflict resolution, and team building strategies.

## Key Topics

- Effective communication techniques
- Conflict resolution strategies
- Team motivation and management
- Mentorship best practices
- Goal setting and accountability

The workshop equipped our leaders with practical skills to better guide and support junior cadets.
