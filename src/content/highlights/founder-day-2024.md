We celebrated Founder's Day with a special ceremony honoring the legacy of Sir William Alexander Smith and the founding of the Boys' Brigade. The event brought together current members, alumni, and church leaders.

## Ceremony Highlights

- Historical presentation on BB origins
- Awards and recognitions ceremony
- Alumni sharing sessions
- Group photo and fellowship
- Special service at church

The celebration reminded us of our rich heritage and continued commitment to the BB mission of nurturing young men in faith, discipline, and service.
